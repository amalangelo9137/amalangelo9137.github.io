Windroid Virtual Life Simulator 3D

The gamer friendly real life like experience

Windroid Virtual Life Simulator aims to be a gamer friendly version of Windroid Sim. In this game, we get to explore a barren land and sustain our lives with the help of the Pseudo Biological Framework (nice name lol). Our mission is to find the Vision UI Module first, so we can access our health stats and enable alerts.

This game is NOT allowed to contain anything from fantasies or our imagination (other than the Pseudo Biological Framework, because we are not literally in the sim. without it, how are we supposed to move with wasd or our mouse?), to help maintain the 1:1 ratio.
